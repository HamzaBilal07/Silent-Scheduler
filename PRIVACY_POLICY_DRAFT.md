# Privacy Policy for Silent Scheduler

Effective date: May 29, 2026

Silent Scheduler is an Android app that helps users automatically enable Do Not Disturb during scheduled events such as prayers, lectures, meetings, study sessions, and other custom events. The app can restore the previous sound mode when the event ends.

## Information Stored on the Device

Silent Scheduler stores the following information locally on the user's device:

- Event names
- Start and end times
- Enabled or disabled state
- Repeat-day settings
- Selected fiqah option
- App preferences
- Runtime automation state needed to restore sound mode

This information is stored locally using Android app storage. It is not sold or used for advertising.

## Location and Prayer Timings

If the user grants location permission, Silent Scheduler uses the phone's latitude and longitude to request daily prayer timings and sunrise from an online prayer timing service.

Location is used only for prayer timing functionality. The app does not use location for advertising, tracking, profiling, or background surveillance.

If location permission is not granted, users can still create and manage manual event schedules.

## Internet Access

Internet access is used to load prayer timing data. The app also includes an offline daily Quran ayah library, so the daily ayah feature does not require an internet connection.

## Permissions

Silent Scheduler may request:

- Do Not Disturb access, to silence the phone during scheduled events.
- Exact alarm access, to run event start and end actions at accurate times.
- Notification permission, to show reminders or important status messages.
- Location permission, to calculate daily prayer timings and sunrise.
- Internet access, to load prayer timing data.
- Boot completed access, to reschedule enabled events after the phone restarts.

## Data Sharing

Silent Scheduler does not sell personal data.

The app may send location coordinates to a prayer timing service only when prayer timings are refreshed. Event schedules, repeat settings, and app preferences remain stored locally on the device.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete individual schedules inside the app. Users can also reset schedules from Settings, clear app storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.

## Children

Silent Scheduler is not designed specifically for children.

This policy may be updated when app features or publishing requirements change. The effective date at the top of this document should be updated whenever the policy changes.
