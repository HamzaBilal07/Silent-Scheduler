# Privacy Policy For Silent Scheduler

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Silent Scheduler helps users silence their phone during configured events, including prayer times, lectures, meetings, and other scheduled activities. The app can restore the previous sound mode after the event ends.

## Data Stored On The Device

The app stores event names, start times, end times, enabled or disabled state, repeat settings, selected fiqah, permission-related settings, and app preferences locally on the user's device.

## Location And Prayer Timings

If the user grants location permission, the app uses the phone's latitude and longitude to fetch daily prayer timings and sunrise from an online prayer timing service. Location is used only for this prayer timing feature. The app does not use location for advertising, tracking, or user profiling.

## Permissions

The app may request:

- Do Not Disturb access, to silence the phone during scheduled events.
- Exact alarm access, to run start and end actions at the selected times.
- Notification permission, to show warnings or optional reminders.
- Location permission, to calculate daily prayer timings and sunrise.
- Internet access, to load prayer timing data.

## Data Sharing

The app does not sell personal data. The app sends location coordinates to the prayer timing service only when needed to calculate daily prayer timings. Event schedules and app settings are stored locally on the device.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete individual schedules or reset schedules inside the app. Users can also remove all local app data by clearing app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.
